Fight or Flight

                    an endless side-scroller survival game

Game Code

Fight or Flight, built using Unity and C# scripts, is my first mobile survival game. I began this project summer of 2020 and finished its first version May 2021. I hope to soon publish the first build to the App Store, after incorporating some new features. Using Krita and Unity, I was able to incorporate my 8-bit style sprites and bring them to life in an endless survival game.

I utilized C# scripts to create joystick controls and touch activated shooting for touch screen devices. Random functions are used to ensure each trial of the game is unique and each sprite has variations in their movement. Also, I made use of different sprite renders and UI sliders to update the health and shoot gauge to show remaining health and reloading of the weapon.

I incorporated a variety of different enemies with unique abilities and different movement capabilities. Each of these enemies utilize the IEnumerator interface with yield to spawn endless enemies until the game is over. To give the feeling of a moving plane, I incorporated an endless background, by moving the clouds across the screen and translating their location back to the starting location once out of the screen.

Fight or Flight offers three scenes: starting menu, gameplay, and scoreboard/restart. These scenes are embedded together seamlessly for simplicity and make the game flow as if it were one scene.

Plans for the Game: I hope to add more features and publish this game. I want to add more stages to the game with new enemies and different backgrounds. Also, I plan to refactor more creating more hierarchy within each sprite to reduce some of the repitition in the code.
